Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

To get started with TON Referral Keeper, simply click the "Start Earning Now" button on our homepage or open our Telegram bot at @TONReferralKeeperBot. Create an account with your email, complete the verification process, and start inviting friends to earn commissions.

How are referral commissions calculated?

We offer a three-tier referral system:

  • Level 1 (Direct referrals): 10% commission on deposits
  • Level 2 (Referral's referrals): 5% commission on deposits
  • Level 3 (Network depth): 2% commission on deposits

Commissions are calculated on each qualifying deposit and paid in TON tokens to your wallet.

When do I receive my earnings?

Referral commissions are automatically calculated and paid to your designated wallet within 24-48 hours of your referral's deposit being confirmed. You can withdraw your earnings at any time via our supported withdrawal methods.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple deposit methods:

  • Direct TON transfers from wallet
  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major coins)
  • Bank transfers (selected regions)

Is there a minimum or maximum deposit?

Minimum deposit: 100 TON or equivalent
Maximum deposit: Unlimited (subject to identity verification for amounts over $10,000)
For more details, contact our support team.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings by:

  • Going to your account dashboard
  • Clicking "Withdraw" in the earnings section
  • Entering your withdrawal amount and wallet address
  • Confirming the transaction

Withdrawals are processed within 2-24 hours depending on blockchain network conditions.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Each user is only permitted to operate a single account. Multiple accounts are considered fraudulent activity and may result in permanent suspension of all accounts and forfeiture of earnings.

How do I report a technical issue?

For technical issues:

  • Email: support@tonreferralkeeper.com
  • Telegram: Join our support channel
  • Include detailed information about the issue and screenshots if possible

What is your refund policy?

Since deposits are made on the blockchain, they are irreversible. However, if you encounter an issue with your account or deposit, our support team can investigate and assist you within our policies. Cryptocurrency transactions cannot be reversed, so please verify all details before confirming any transfer.

Are there any fees?

TON Keeper does not charge account fees or service fees for using our platform. However, standard blockchain network fees may apply to withdrawals depending on the cryptocurrency used. Credit card deposits may be subject to processing fees as disclosed at checkout.

How do I secure my account?

To secure your account:

  • Use a strong, unique password (mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters)
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Never share your account credentials or recovery phrases
  • Regularly update your password
  • Verify phishing attempts - we never ask for passwords via email
  • Use a secure, updated device with antivirus software

Still Need Help?

Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or issues. Don't hesitate to reach out.
